# About Shadow Bean

While most Chattini are active during the day, Shadow Bean emerges when the sun sets. Don't let their mysterious nature fool you - underneath that dark exterior beats a heart just as caring as any other community member.

## Abilities

- **Night Watch**: Monitors the community during late hours
- **Meme Sniper**: Drops the perfect meme at exactly the right moment
- **Silent Support**: Helps others without seeking recognition
- **Time Zone Master**: Somehow always online, no matter what time it is

## Background

Nobody knows exactly when Shadow Bean first appeared in the community. Some say they were there from the very beginning, watching from the shadows. Others believe Shadow Bean is a time traveler, explaining their uncanny ability to be present across all time zones.

What we do know is that Shadow Bean has become an integral part of the Chattini family, representing the night owls and late-night grinders who keep the community alive 24/7.

## Personality

Shadow Bean speaks in cryptic messages and rare emoji combinations. They have a dry sense of humor that catches people off guard, but their loyalty to the community is absolute. Despite their mysterious persona, they're known for sliding into DMs to check on people who seem down.
